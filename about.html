Building Dreams Since 2009

Dom Expert was founded with a simple vision: to make the New York real estate experience transparent, personal, and rewarding. What started as a small boutique agency in Manhattan has grown into one of the city's most trusted real estate firms.

Over 15 years later, we've helped thousands of clients find their perfect homes, make smart investments, and navigate the complexities of NYC's property market. Our team combines deep local knowledge with a commitment to integrity that sets us apart.

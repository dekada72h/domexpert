About This Property

Welcome to this stunning luxury apartment situated in the heart of Manhattan's prestigious Upper East Side. This beautifully renovated residence features three spacious bedrooms, two modern bathrooms, and a private terrace with breathtaking views of the city skyline. The open-concept living and dining area is bathed in natural light thanks to oversized windows, and the chef's kitchen boasts premium stainless steel appliances, quartz countertops, and custom cabinetry.

Residents enjoy access to world-class amenities including a fully equipped fitness center, rooftop lounge, 24-hour doorman service, and on-site laundry facilities. The building is pet-friendly and offers private storage units. Located steps away from Central Park, Museum Mile, top-rated restaurants, and excellent public transportation, this apartment offers the perfect blend of luxury living and urban convenience.
